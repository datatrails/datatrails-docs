RKVST Basics will quickly get you up and running by guiding you through:

  • Creating your first Asset
  • Recording lifecycle Events on your Asset

It gives simple but sufficient overview of the core concepts of RKVST to get you going. For more complete coverage of the core concepts please refer to the concepts section.
